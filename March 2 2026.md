# General

## Sentences
- be direct to the point, concise
- ensure coherence of sentences in paragraph
- describing your system as "revolutionizing" a process is egocentric and cringe
- as much as possible, do not cite a paper more than once
- paper contents must be double-spaced, left-aligned, but don't use CTRL+A to make them double-spaced because it will mess up the title page and such
- ensure clarity; avoid using "the authors" as it is unclear whether it refers to you or the researcher in the study you are citing
- avoid using "several" because it is common with AI-generated sentences?
- avoid putting the predicate at the beginning of a sentence; ex: To improve blah blah, the authors blah blah blah blah X
- ensure tone is not robotic

## Figures
- make sure figures are properly labeled by adding a caption* to image, format is "Fig. #" not "Figure #" (APA)
- every image in the paper must be included in table of figures* labeled "List of Figures"
- figures must be left-aligned

## Tables
- format = font is 9 black, border on table top, below heading, and table bottom, heading is centered
- **do not paste images as table**
- every table must be included in table of figures* labeled "List of Tables"
