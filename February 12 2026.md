## Sample Outline of RRL: 
  - context of the project (¿what is it for? business, agriculture, socio-cultural)
  - conceptual foundation/dealing? (ex. use of smart devices, optimization of manual processes)
  - uniqueness of application (ex. ui/ux principles, methods used to fulfil features like dashboard and exported files)
  - platform/tools used (ex. devop, agile, ¿Laravel?, front-end back-end stack) ¿with justification? (research characteristics of end users)
  - why web and mobile application? (what technologies used in articles read)
  - no strict rule for the title of the subheadings but make sure it is coherent with what the paragraph actually describes
  - how to cite an app for the related system?
  - similar system doesn't have to just be in terms of function but also in terms of aesthetic (ex. dashboard you will copy)
